How to setup a meeting

This first video is the most important - how to setup a meeting.
To setup a meeting from SCRATCH, you have to add a Group, then setup a Location for the meeting.
You can setup different locations for the same group if a group meets in more than one place.
Once you've setup a group and a location for it, you can add as many meetings as you want for that group
and location. Setup a Group, Location and Meeting
